Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 mins

Servings: 4

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add milk, eggs, oil, and vanilla. Stir until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 35 minutes. Let cool before serving.
